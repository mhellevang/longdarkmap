    The Long Dark Interactive Map

    An interactive map for The Long Dark survival game by Hinterland Studio. Explore all 21 regions of Great Bear Island including Mystery Lake, Coastal Highway, Pleasant Valley, Desolation Point, Timberwolf Mountain, Forlorn Muskeg, Broken Railroad, Bleak Inlet, Hushed River Valley, Mountain Town, Ash Canyon, Blackrock, Forsaken Airfield, Sundered Pass, Zone of Contamination, Crumbling Highway, Far Range Branch Line, Transfer Pass, Ravine, Winding River & Carter Hydro Dam, and Keeper's Pass.

    Features: search 364 named locations across all regions, zoomable and pannable detail maps for every region, find the nearest forge, workbench, or ammunition workbench from any region, deep-link sharing, and full offline support. No server required — works as a single HTML file.

    Crafting stations: 6 forges, 45 workbenches, and 2 ammunition workbenches mapped across all regions.

    Map artwork by community creators Krueger and HokuOwl, hosted on Steam. This is a fan-made project, not affiliated with Hinterland Studio.
